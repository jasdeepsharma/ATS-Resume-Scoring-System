Job Description
Position: Senior Software Engineer

Company: TechCorp Inc.

Location: San Francisco, CA (Remote Friendly)

Job Description:

TechCorp Inc. is at the forefront of cloud-based enterprise solutions, and we are looking for a seasoned Senior Software Engineer to join our dynamic and growing team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for building scalable, high-performance applications and a proven track record of delivering robust software. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and deploying new features for our flagship product, working with a modern tech stack.

Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and maintain high-quality, reusable, and reliable code.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.

Work with product managers to translate requirements into technical specifications.

Ensure the performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications.

Identify and correct bottlenecks and fix bugs.

Help maintain code quality, organization, and automation.

Participate in agile development processes, including sprint planning, daily stand-ups, and retrospectives.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

5+ years of professional software development experience.

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.

Strong proficiency in Python and experience with frameworks like Django or Flask.

Expertise in front-end technologies, including React, JavaScript, and CSS.

Solid experience with AWS services (EC2, S3, Lambda, RDS).

Proficiency with containerization technologies like Docker and orchestration with Kubernetes.

Experience with relational databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, MySQL) and SQL.

Strong understanding of Git and CI/CD pipelines (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI).

Preferred Skills:

Master's degree in Computer Science.

Experience with Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using Terraform.

Familiarity with monitoring and logging tools (e.g., Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack).

Experience working in an Agile/Scrum development process.

Knowledge of microservices architecture.
